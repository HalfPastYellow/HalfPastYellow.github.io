We're hiring!

At Half Past Yellow we strongly believe that interesting, talented people will tend to make great, innovative experiences. As a small team and a tight-knit group, we generally look for someone that can bring fresh ideas to the games we make and join us in challenging each other to be better.

We're a diverse, international bunch with greatly varying interests and are looking for another unique voice to help shape the things we make, could it be yours?

All positions are ON-SITE in Copenhagen, Denmark. However, we can offer flexible hours and a possibility for partial work-from-home if desired.

Games Producer

As a Games Producer, you will be responsible for overseeing the development process of our games from start to finish, ensuring that they are delivered on time, within budget, and to a high level of quality. You will work closely with management to plan productions, scope projects, and manage the development team to create the best production practices for everyone at Half Past Yellow.

Responsibilities

- Collaborate with every department, driving them to perform their best and ensure that project goals are aligned

- Create project schedules and budgets and ensure that they are met

- Evaluate current project risks and consider contingencies

- Run planning meetings and attend team meetings to assure development stays on track

- Engage with and foster our fledgling community

June 2023
