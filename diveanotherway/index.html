About

Synchronise your diving with a friend by throwing your phones into the air to receive a score based on technicality, jump height and synchronicity. Dive Another Way is a local multiplayer game played using mobile phones as input devices while gameplay is displayed on a big screen.

Dive Another Way was created at Exile Game Jam in 2015. We continued to polish the game as a team and have showed it off at Amaze Festival in Berlin in 2016 and at local events in Copenhagen.
