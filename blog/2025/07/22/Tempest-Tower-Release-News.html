Tempest Tower Launch News

Tempest Tower will leave Early Access on the 19th of August

Posted by Max on July 22, 2025 · 2 mins read

Strap yourself in because we have a truckload of news to drop on you this week. We have dropped the second Major Update for Tempest Tower, and it comes with news of a 1.0 launch date.

Major Update 02

We have 3 big additions this update; Level Challenges, Stat Tracking, and a bunch of new Upgrades. If you ever wanted to recover health by stomping on enemies or transform your Dynamo Switch into an AOE lightning machine, then this is the update for you!

Each level now has 3 challenges associated with it. These challenges can be completed in isolation, ticking them off one by one, or you can try and take out multiple in one go. Each challenge you clear will upgrade the Trophy displayed by that level portal. Can you get the elusive Platinum Trophy on every level of the game?!
