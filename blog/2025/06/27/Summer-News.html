A whole new world: Touch Grass Update for Tempest Tower

I hope you are ready to touch some grass, because this update brings you to a brand new world: The Garden! The Garden world features a new enemy type to deal with as well as new hazards and items.

This update also contains a new and improved level selection hub. This new world space adds a boatload of charm to the game and we will be expanding on this space as we go. The keen-eyed among you will recognise some familiar objects from our previous titles.

As of posting, Tempest Tower is on a Steam Summer Sale discount! Tell your friends!

Time on Frog Island - Steam Summer Sale

Let's not forget our amphibian friends! Our fresh, froggy title, Time on Frog Island is also on sale this summer! Grabbit now!

We Released Spectacle: Worlds Unseen!

In other news, we've done gone and released everything we have on Spectacle so that it wasn't just sitting on a dusty old hard drive until the end of time!

Spectacle: Worlds Unseen is a cancelled Half Past Yellow project from 2023. We outlined why we had to cancel it in a previous blog post here.

We hope that you give it a spin for a peek at the full-game we were setting out to build. If you liked what you saw, come let us know on our Discord!
