About

You are the crew of a space exploration vessel returning to Erf from a mission in the far depths of space. Unfortunately, some of you are infected with an incurable and highly contagious space virus that must not be allowed to reach planet Erf!

Each player is either Infected or Healthy. The goal of an Infected person is to convince the others that they are Healthy so they can make it to Erf and infect the population. The goal of the Healthy players is to correctly identify who is infected and throw them out the airlock before reaching Erf.

Out The Airlock is a hidden information game created at Exile Game Jam in 2015.
