About

Stream Train is a co-op adventure where up to 6 players livestream their trip through the Uncanny Valley. Grow your audience by taunting cryptid creatures, doing dangerous stunts, and pranking your friends.

Chatters will react to whatever they see on the livestream, pose challenges, and make requests. They will also tell you when they are bored. If you keep the content flowing, chatters will keep the donations flowing.
