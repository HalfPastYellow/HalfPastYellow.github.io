Half Past Yellow is a small independent game development studio based in Copenhagen, Denmark founded by four close friends: Casper, Gian, Max, and Remy. Since 2017, Half Past Yellow has released 3 commercial titles and won 4 awards.

With a strong background in rapid-prototyping and game-jamming, we strive to constantly experiment and push the boundaries of what makes games fun and engaging across all sorts of different genres and art styles.

We released Time on Frog Island for PC and all major consoles in 2022 and Tempest Tower on PC in 2025. We are currently working on Stream Train, a co-op horror game about livestreaming your adventures exploring an abandoned railway line. Stay tuned for updates!
