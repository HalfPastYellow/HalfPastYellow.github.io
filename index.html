About

- cool games by cool people -

Half Past Yellow is a Copenhagen based indie studio made up of three close friends: Gian Dbeis, Max Wrighton, and Remy Stuurwold. The company was unofficially started in 2014 when we began to regularly jam together under the Half Past Yellow name. Now that we have founded the company we have began working full time on our first commercial game: Tiny Tomb

We enjoy making small quirky games and continue to game jam together whenever we can. We have showcased projects across Europe and in America at events such as Amaze in Berlin and Google I/O in Mountain View.
